Privacy and Identity on Social Media: A Survey

This survey is seeks to understand people's privacy expectations and strategies for maintaining privacy online.

Participation is purely anonymous and voluntary, and you may ask us to withdraw your data at any time, by providing us your unique randomly generated username.
